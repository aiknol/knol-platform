Target Use Cases

🤖

AI Assistants & Chatbots

Enable AI assistants to remember user preferences, past conversations, and learned behaviors across sessions. Personalized experiences without re-explaining context.

🏥

Healthcare AI

Patient history tracking with PII redaction. Maintain longitudinal health records while ensuring HIPAA compliance through built-in privacy controls.

📚

Education & Tutoring

Adaptive learning systems that track student progress, learning styles, and knowledge gaps. Procedural memory captures how each student learns best.

💼

Enterprise Knowledge Base

Organizational knowledge capture from Slack, email, and meetings. Knowledge graph maps relationships between people, projects, and decisions.

🛒

E-commerce Personalization

Remember customer preferences, browsing patterns, and purchase history. Semantic memory consolidates product affinities for intelligent recommendations.

⚖️

Legal & Compliance

Case history tracking with temporal knowledge graphs. Track how regulations, interpretations, and relationships evolve over time with bi-temporal modeling.

Go-to-Market Strategy

Phase 1

Developer Adoption

Open-source the core, build community. Target individual developers and small startups building AI applications. Focus on documentation, tutorials, and GitHub presence.

Phase 2

Cloud Launch

Launch managed cloud service (Starter + Pro tiers). Reduce friction from self-hosting. Target teams that want memory without infrastructure management.

Phase 3

Enterprise Sales

Direct sales to enterprises needing on-premise deployment, SSO, custom connectors, and dedicated support. Target healthcare, finance, and legal verticals.
