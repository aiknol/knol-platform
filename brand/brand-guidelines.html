Brand guidelines for Knol — memory infrastructure for AI. Minimal, elegant, and built for developers.

Logo

The Knol logo consists of an icon mark, a wordmark, and a combination mark. The icon is a geometric K with violet knowledge nodes at its endpoints.

Clear Space

Maintain clear space equal to the icon height on all sides. Minimum size: 120px wide (combo), 16px (icon only).

Logo Rules

Do

Use the provided SVG files at any scale. The logo is always lowercase.

Do

Use the white variant on dark backgrounds and the dark variant on light backgrounds.

Don't

Rotate, skew, add shadows, gradients, or effects to the logo.

Don't

Display the wordmark in uppercase, title case, or change the typeface.

Usage guidelines

How to apply the Knol brand consistently across digital products, documentation, and marketing materials.

Voice & tone

Technical but never cold. Confident but never arrogant. Write like engineers explaining something they built with care — direct, precise, and occasionally understated. Let the product speak through clean documentation.

Do

"Knol extracts entities and relationships from conversations, building a knowledge graph that improves retrieval over time."

Don't

"Knol is an AMAZING AI-powered memory platform that will REVOLUTIONIZE how your apps remember things!!!"
